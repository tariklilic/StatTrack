Stat Track is a website that allows you to check your and other players League of Legends profiles. It allows you to track statistics, winrates, levels and divisions.

Stat Track is created by 3 University students: Sandi Stokanovic, Dzenis Kajevic and Tarik Lilic.

No, Stat Track is not a Riot Official website. It is a website created by 3 students, to track League of Legends players statistics.

Stat Track is free to use and currently has no charges.

Matches are limited due to Riot Games API limitations. We will be looking to increase the number in the future.

Stat Track currently only tracks League of Legends statistics. We will be looking to add more Riot Games to Stat Track.

We were always big fans of Riot Games. We used game trackers on daily bases, therefore we got the idea of making our own Stat Tracker.

Currently there are only 3 regions on Stat Track: Eune, Euw and NA. We will be looking to add more regions in the future

Stat Track account is free to create and to use.

Stat Track is 100% safe to use. We won't have access to your Riot profile.
